Our Philosophy

Light. Warm. Wonderfully you.

We're drawn to the beauty of modern farmhouse design — the clean lines, the natural textures, the way a well-designed space feels both fresh and lived-in at the same time. We call it city-country: the polish of contemporary design with the soul of home.

Every project begins with understanding how your family actually lives. Light matters. Flow matters. The way you feel when you walk through the front door matters most of all.

We source furniture and materials with intention — pieces that are beautiful, durable, and worth passing down.

The Designer

Designing spaces that hold your family's story

I'm Ruby Chang, the principal designer behind Redrose Design. I've spent over a decade helping families across North Texas create homes that are both beautifully designed and genuinely livable.

My style is rooted in modern farmhouse — clean, light, and bright with warm natural textures and thoughtful details. I love the city-country balance: spaces that feel curated without feeling cold, and cozy without feeling cluttered.

Based in McKinney, Texas. Serving the greater DFW area and available for virtual consultations nationwide.
